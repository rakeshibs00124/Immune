More Info

Guam Immunization Program provides useful information.

Having expert information by your side is important to prevent diseases and to stay healthy. Our goal is to keep our island community informed about immunization. Let us know how we can help.

USEFUL WEBSITES:
We have a list of useful websites which we highly recommend. Please get your information only from official sources. We recommend our list of official websites.
> View the list

VIDEOS & MEDIA:
There is a lot of information out there, especially videos on popular platforms such as YouTube. Be careful and watch only validated information from official sources.
> View useful videos

QUESTIONS & ANSWERS: The Immunization Program does not give shots. However, we can surely help guide you to community partners and medical providers. If you have questions, we have answers.
